To get a picture of what happened to SPARK - before and after the community takeover took place - you'll find all the necessary info in this BITCOINTALK THREAD.

  • 25.10.2014 - this is the last statement from the former dev. it's pretty close to the moment when the first issue started that must have convinced him to simply get lost: bitcointalk.org > 826767.msg9329478
  • 29.10.2014 - a few days and some fud and wild speculation later, as well some serious efforts to set the mess straight already had taken place. this is when we've got the first fixed wallet: bitcointalk.org > 826767.msg9364643.
    this moment went a little bit out of focus with the running discussion and all the hassle, but the 29.10.2014 should well be considered the Official Date Of The Takeover.

Tech Yadda Yadda:

Spark is a PoW/PoS-based cryptocurrency, now in PoS phase.

  • Algorithm: X11
  • Block Time: 60 Seconds
  • PoW Reward: 200 SPARK reducing to 100 SPARK at block 7200
  • PoW Timeframe: 14400 Blocks (~10 Days)
  • Total PoW: ~2,160,000 SPARK
  • PoS Start: Block 14000
  • PoS Interest: 17.5%
  • Confirmations: 10, Maturity: 100
  • Minimum stake age: 12 Hours - Unlimited Max
  • P2P port: 16666
  • RPC port: 16667
